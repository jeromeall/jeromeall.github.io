About me

Welcome! My name is Jerome and I'm a full stack web developer. Although I've had many years working on the business side of online technologies, I only recently made the switch to website development. My past experiences working with software, as well as my increased interest in product development have encouraged me to pursue a career as a developer.

We are part of generation that is not afraid to challenge the norms, a generation that is changing the way we live through technology. My dream is to push this generation forward by creating products that make a real positive difference in the lives of its users. I hope that by the time you reach the end of this page, you'll have a better idea of how I plan to achieve this dream. Enjoy!

I wish to be part of a team where I can learn as much as I can contribute.

My Skills

The ladder approach is the one I'm following in my path as a developer. I am always seeking to learn new languages and technologies as a way to explore innovative solutions to complex problems.

Coupled with these technical abilities, I have acquired many valuable skills throughout my career that make me a stronger developer.
